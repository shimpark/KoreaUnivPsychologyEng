Research topic

Consumer & Advertising Psychology?

The Department of Consumer and Advertising Psychology at Korea University was established to study consumer psychology for the first time in Korea, starting from the concept that in order to understand consumer behavior, you must first understand the psychology of consumers in depth. Top faculty and researchers are studying the ever-changing consumption phenomenon and the underlying consumption process based on psychological theory. We are conducting research focusing on the use of new technologies, human-artificial intelligence interaction, ethical development of technology, and sustainable consumption for the improvement of the quality of personal life and the sustainable development of society. Through such active research, we aim to not only improve the academic understanding of consumer behavior and psychology, but also suggest practical and policy directions. Currently, one professor is leading the Consumer and Advertising Psychology Lab at Korea University.
