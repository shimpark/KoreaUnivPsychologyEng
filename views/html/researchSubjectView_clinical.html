Clinical & Counseling Psychology?

The Clinical and Counseling Psychology (CCP) program at Korea University, the largest in Korea, provides systematic clinical and counseling training and top-notch education and research. The CCP program offers advanced training which integrates the scientist-practitioner model and the counseling psychology training model. The CCP program at Korea University focuses on the following major areas for education and research.

  • more

    Young Gun Ko PhD

    As a positive clinical psychologist, I currently conduct research on the psychology of happiness, the psychology of love, 'happiness gamification,' which promotes happiness using game elements, and 'flourish,' which means the state of mental prosperity of humans. We are working on a task to develop AI-based digital psychotherapy using psychological diagnosis, which is one of the greatest strengths of clinical psychology.

    Digital Psychotherapy & AI, Psychology of Happiness, Psychology of Love, Psychological Testing, Psychodiagnosis
  • more

    Eun Joo Yang PhD

    Career is one of the most important areas in the human experience. It is a matter of how an individual designs one’s life, rather than just finding a job. From the perspective of life-span development, we study career development with an emphasis on the intersections of individual differences, contextual influences, and developmental stages. The particular research topics include the career development trajectories and adaptability in the modern society characterized by increased uncertainty and the roles of cultural background and minority identity on career development, 

    Counseling Psychology, Career Development, Multiculturalism
  • more

    Ki Hong Choi Associate Professor

    Evidence-based psychotherapy and psychosocial rehabilitation are developed, and effectiveness is studied. We are developing psychological evaluation tools for the Korean population, and developing and distributing in-person and non-face-to-face psychological service platforms. We developed a face-to-face/non-face-to-face hybrid technology that combines ICT technology and machine learning technology with psychological services, and provide services to public institutions and industries.

    Severe mental illness, personality assessment, psychotherapy outcome trials, development of psychological assessment, ICT based psychological service development
  • more

    Ji-Won Hur Assistant Professor

    Focusing on emotional processing and social cognitive function, we are exploring the pathology of depression, suicide, and self-injury without suicidal intention through surveys, behavioral experiments, and brain imaging research, and we continue to develop and demonstrate digital therapeutics related to this. We are also conducting research on the link between poverty and mental health.

    Psychopathology, Psychotherapy, Digital Therapeutics, NSSI, Poverty
