Psychology is

Psychology is the science of behavior and the mind and is based on knowledge of the humanities, Social problems, and natural sciences. It is a discipline that provides the most objective standard for understanding humans.

The future society wants talented people who can provide creative and
scientific solutions to social problems in various fields.

As a response to the demands of the future and the problems at hand,
the curriculum has been changed from an academic-oriented curriculum to a social problem-centered one.

Psychology training to solve the challenges of future society

  • AI

    As new social and ethical issues emerge as AI development accelerates, an understanding and proactive response to the role and interaction of humans and AI are needed.

  • Fusion

    An educational system is needed for convergent talents who pursue expertise through a multidisciplinary approach that crosses the boundaries between academic fields.

  • Diversity

    In order to play a leading role in the process of rapid internationalization and technological development, it is necessary to cultivate the ability to generate new ideas and understand the phenomenon in multiple ways..

  • Continuous possibilities

    For the sustainable development of future society, there is a need for a solution that promotes harmonious prosperity and growth of individuals, organizations, and the environment, not just the strategy of delaying the depletion of resources.

  • Communication

    Communication skills to effectively communicate one's thoughts and arguments to others and the ability to empathize with others' emotions are needed for social adaptation and harmony..

  • The curriculum centered on the traditional academic system is dismantled, and the curriculum centered on an innovative social theme is transformed into a reorganization.
